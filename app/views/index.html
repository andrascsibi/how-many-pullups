Counting made easy

The 1000 Pull-up Challenge

In 2014 I accepted a challenge to perform 1000 strict tactical pull-ups in one month. Counting to one thousand is a difficult task. You can keep count on paper, but I tend to lose pieces of paper. Maintaining an Excel sheet is tedious. Those full-blown habit tracker apps? Life is just too short for them.

Pull-App is simple, cross-platform, and social

So I created Pull-App. When I'm done with a set of pull-ups, I press exactly one button, on any device, and it's stored in my training journal forever. Also, everybody can see my challenge board, so my friends can keep me accountable for finishing the challenge.

Track your own sets and reps with Pull-App!

Use it for your own fitness challenge, can be as simple as 100 pushups in a week. Heck, even standing up from your desk ten times a day can be a good fitness challenge in itself.
